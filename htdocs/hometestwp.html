Depression and Anxiety:
How About You?

Do you often feel a bit down, like you just want to give up on life? If so, then you may be experiencing depression.
If you are unsure, however, then you may click the button below to take a short test if you have depression and/or anxiety.
Depression Test
Note that we are not professionals when it comes to mental health, so we advise you not to rely on the test and seek medical help if you do feel that you have depression.
