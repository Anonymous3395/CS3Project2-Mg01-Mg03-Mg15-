Depression and Anxiety:
How About You?

The test below is simple. You will be shown some situations, and you'll have to choose on how often you can relate to that situation. After that, your results will be displayed. Don't feel pressured though. You can take your time here. There is no time limit. Click the button below to begin the test. Just choose either

1 = Not at all/Rarely.
2 = Sometimes
3 = Often
4 = Mostly.
